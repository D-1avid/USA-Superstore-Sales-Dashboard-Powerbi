# USA-Superstore-Sales-Dashboard-Powerbi
This project presents an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed in Microsoft Power BI to analyze sales performance across the United States using the Superstore Sales Dataset (2014–2017).
The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through data modeling, DAX calculations, KPI reporting, and interactive visualizations that support executive decision-making.

Project Objectives
The dashboard answers key business questions such as:
Which states generate the highest profit?
Which cities place the most orders?
Which products generate the most revenue?
Who are the highest-value customers?
How has sales performance changed over time?
What are the overall business KPIs?

Key Business Insights
Sales increased steadily between 2014 and 2017.
California generated the highest overall profit.
New York City recorded the highest number of customer orders.
Office Supplies accounted for the largest share of total orders.
Canon imageCLASS Copier generated the highest sales among all products.
The business maintained positive profitability across multiple regions.

Business Recommendations
Increase inventory before peak sales periods.
Prioritize marketing in high-performing states.
Expand sales strategies around top-selling products.
Improve performance in lower-profit states through targeted promotions.
Develop customer loyalty initiatives focused on high-value customers.
